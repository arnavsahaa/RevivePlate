Our Mission

At RevivePlate, we believe no good food should go to waste. We rescue surplus meals from hostels, restaurants, and events, repackage them with care, and offer them at affordable prices—because everyone deserves access to delicious, budget-friendly food.

Weekly Food Donation Drive
